Mondrian's
Dream

Mondrian has had a dream, a dream in which it appear a canvas filled with color. When he wakes up, he wants to paint the piece he dreamt about but he can not remember the colors that were in it.
In this game you will have an opportunity to see for a few seconds the art piece that Mondrian sees in his dreams which and you will have to try to memorize it. Then, you will have to help Mondrian to locate the colors into their designated space on the blank canvas so that you can help him replicate the art piece he dreamt about.
Can you help Mondrian recreate the canvas of his dreams?

Mondrian's dream will disappear in   7   seconds

  • 1. Select a color
  • 2. Click on the square
  • 3. Choose another color
  • 4. When you have finished, click "I'm done!"
  • !! Remember, you are allowed to check the hints just 3 times!
